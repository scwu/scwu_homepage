TrialHire

Trialhire is a recruiting platform for technical interviews that helps give recruiters technical analytics on candidates before the phone screen. We provide online technical interviews with technology that enables playback of the interview process as well as cheating detection software and a dashboard to manage candidates and positions.

LessonHub

LessonHub is a GitHub for teachers that makes it easy for teachers to create, share and collaborate on course curriculums. We make it possible to create entire lesson plans, edit them in rich text and then make them searchable, as well as making it simple to "fork" a lesson and make whichever changes you'd like.

Knock Factor Authentication

Knock Factor Authentication is a YHack project, and then a hackNY reunion demo, that makes entering your two factor authentication as easy as "knocking" on your phone. Linked with Google Auth, a server on your computer running in the program and a Chrome extension - no more typing in annoying codes. Completed web portion of code.

3D Text Generator

3D Text Generator is a PennApps project meant just for fun. We used 3D.js to create a tool that made it easy to build 3D text graphics with customizations that also animate, and then easily export this text as a GIF. During the hackathon, I designed the website and wrote the front-end code that enabled customization of fonts and colors.

Penn Women in Computer Science website

The Women in Computer Science site was built in Django and integrated both the Facebook and Google Calendar API to make it easy to keep the site up to date. I utilized Django Admin to make entering new event flyers and posting Facebook events very simple. I worked full stack and designed the site.

Dropbox Dinner Invitation

The Dropbox Dinner Invitation idea was formulated when Dropbox asked WiCS to do an event. At the time, I had been obsessively interested in letter press and wedding invitation and decided to design and write an invite under this specific theme, gaining inspiration for what we see today as a formalized print invitation. I ripped out Google Form code to integrate it into the invite.

When2Meet Version 2.0

When2Meet is a webapp built to decide what and when to do with your friends when you are trying to meet up. It uses a combination of a voting system and people's availability as decided via Google Calendar to determine the most popular time and place to do a "hangout". The final result is emailed out to all participants. Built in AngularJS and Node. Did design and contributed full-stack.

Penn WiCS Time Capsule

The Women in Computer Science Time Capsule was built for a project to bury a Penn Engineering time capsule within the Engineering building. I wanted to build something that represented our generation in both content and medium. I wrote and designed a website that represented our organization's hopes for the future and saved it onto a USB drive to be buried.

Transparency.io

Transparency.io is built in Django to increase corporate backing transparency. It is a search engine that allows you to search for corporations and then find out how much money was donated from that particular organization to different poliical parties, bills and organizations. It is broken down in graphical charts - made with Chart.JS.

Penn Computer Science 2013 Summer Plans

Penn Summer Plans was built in Django, D3.js and BackboneJS and utilized the LinkedIn API to extrapolate and visualize summer and full time plans for Penn CS students in 2013. I visualized distribution of students throughout the country as well as average company size and impact of industries on each other, etc.
