Knock Factor Authentication

Knock Factor Authentication is a YHack project, and then a hackNY reunion demo, that makes entering your two factor authentication as easy as "knocking" on your phone. Linked with Google Auth, a server on your computer running in the program and a Chrome extension - no more typing in annoying codes. Completed web portion of code.

3D Text Generator

3D Text Generator is a PennApps project meant just for fun. We used 3D.js to create a tool that made it easy to build 3D text graphics with customizations that also animate, and then easily export this text as a GIF. During the hackathon, I designed the website and wrote the front-end code that enabled customization of fonts and colors.

Penn Women in Computer Science website

The Women in Computer Science site was built in Django and integrated both the Facebook and Google Calendar API to make it easy to keep the events and news up to date and integrate the site with our social media. I utilized Django Admin to make entering new event flyers and posting Facebook events very simple. I did both design and development for this.

Dropbox Dinner Invitation

The Dropbox Dinner Invitation idea was formulated when Dropbox asked WiCS to do an event. At the time, I had been obsessively interested in letter press and wedding invitation and decided to design and write an invite under this specific theme, gaining inspiration for what we see today as a formalized print invitation. I ripped out Google Form code to integrate it into the invite.
